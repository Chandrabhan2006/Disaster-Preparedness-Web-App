❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🧰 What should I include in an emergency kit?

✅ Water 💧, Food 🍞, Flashlight 🔦, Batteries 🔋, First Aid Kit 🩹, Medications 💊, Important Documents 📂.

🌐 Where can I find real-time disaster alerts?

📡 Government websites, 📱 official apps, and 📻 local radio/TV broadcasts.

🌍 What is the safest action during an earthquake?

🙇 Drop to the ground, 🛡️ take cover under sturdy furniture, and ✊ hold on until shaking stops.
